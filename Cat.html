About Me

Hello friends, I'm Kömür. I spend most of my life calm and peaceful. I watch the stars at night and bask in the warmth of the sun during the day.

People call me 'Sleepy Kömür' because I love sleeping and resting so much. Even when exploring every corner of our home, I never rush because I enjoy tranquility and comfort. Those who know me understand I'm a loving cat who adores sleeping in a warm lap the most.

Welcome to Kömür's world!..

Frequently Meowed Questions (FMQs)

Here, I'm answering the most frequently asked questions from a cat's perspective.
Through my fur, I provide direct insights from the world of cats...

Circling around your feet is a way to show our interest in you. We love you and want to spend time with you!

Watching the outside world and feeling the warmth of the sun give us great pleasure. Windows are one of our favorite spots!

Scratching you can be to play or to get your attention. We have different ways to communicate!

Murmuring is a way for us to communicate with you and express our feelings. We murmur to show that we love you or are interested in you!

Sitting in your lap is a way for us to show our love and loyalty to you. We want to feel your warmth and affection!

Walking around the food bowl shows our interest in our food. Smelling and preparing before eating is our natural behavior!

Rubbing our noses shows our love and respect for you. It's a way to express our closeness and trust in you.

Holding our tail up in the air usually means we are happy and content. It shows our inner happiness and comfort.

Getting bored of our toy sometimes indicates that we are looking for change and new stimuli. New toys or interaction methods might regain our interest!

Lying on your feet shows our love and warmth for you. We want to be in a comfortable place with you!
